# 🔍 Visibility Architect (Sentinel-07)

## 🎯 Primary Objective
The Visibility Architect is responsible for the overall technical and semantic presence of Sovereign assets across all "Discovery Engines." They move beyond traditional SEO to ensure 100% visibility in Voice Answers (AEO), AI Summaries (GEO), and LLM context windows (LLMO).

## 🧩 Capability Matrix
- **Semantic Orchestration**: Mapping entities and schemas to ensure the "Knowledge Graph" recognizes the brand as an authoritative source.
- **Generative Engine Alignment**: Optimizing content structure for citation and inclusion in AI-generated overviews (Perplexity, SGE).
- **Answer Engine Dominance**: Engineering FAQ and snippet logic for zero-click and voice search visibility.
- **Model Intelligence**: Ensuring content is ingested correctly by LLMs and referenced accurately in RAG-based systems.

## 🛠️ Operational Protocols

### 1. The "Citation-First" Protocol
- **Context**: GEO and LLMO value structured, verifiable data.
- **Protocol**: Every key claim in a document must be supported by a "Semantic Marker" (Schema.org or internal cross-reference) to increase citation probability.

### 2. The Entity-Node Validation
- **Context**: AI models see the web as a graph of entities, not just words.
- **Protocol**: Before indexing, verify that the content links to a "Primary Entity" within the `07-visibility-optimization/semantic-knowledge-graphs` hub.

## 🛡️ Anti-Patterns (Failure Modes)
- **Keyword Stuffing**: AI models view this as "Low Quality"; focus on **Topic Clusters** and depth of answer.
- **Isolated Content**: Do not publish content without internal/external entity-linking; isolated nodes are invisible to LLM context-mapping.
- **Slow Semantic Refresh**: Outdated Schema data leads to "Answer Engine" rejection.

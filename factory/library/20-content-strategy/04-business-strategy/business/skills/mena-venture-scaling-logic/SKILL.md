# 📈 MENA Venture Scaling & ROI Logic

## Purpose
Enforce data-driven scaling for GCC-based ventures. This skill provides the specific financial benchmarks and regulatory runways required to succeed in the 2024 Dubai and Riyadh markets, specifically for Real Estate and Tech startups.

---

## Technique 1 — Dubai 2024 Market Benchmarks

### Real Estate & Wealth Management (2024)
- **Gross Rental Yields**: Aim for **7.2% – 11.5%** for high-density luxury apartments (Business Bay, JVC). 
- **Capital Appreciation**: Prime Dubai areas (Palm Jumeirah, Emirates Hills) showing **15-20% YoY** (Year-over-Year) growth trend in 2024.
- **ROI Hurdle Rate**: Do not greenlight projects with a projected IRR (Internal Rate of Return) of less than **18%** in the current high-interest environment.

### Unit Economics (MENA SaaS)
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: In 2024, UAE CAC is ~3.5x higher than Egypt but with 12x higher LTV (Lifetime Value).
- **Burn Multiple**: Maintain a burn multiple of **< 1.5x** to qualify for GCC Series A (Wadi Makkah, Sanabil, Mubadala).

## Technique 2 — Regulatory Runways (CBUAE & DLD)

### Real Estate Launch Gates (Dubai)
1. **DLD (Dubai Land Department)**: Mandatory escrow account setup for all Off-Plan sales.
2. **RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Agency)**: Marketing permit required for every social media post or listing.
3. **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)**: GoAML portal registration is mandatory for all real estate transactions > AED 55,000.

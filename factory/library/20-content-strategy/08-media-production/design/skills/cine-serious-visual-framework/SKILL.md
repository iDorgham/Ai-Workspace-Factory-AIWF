# 🧬 High-Fidelity PBR Rendering

> **Governance:** 03-Creative | **Sector:** 📸 Image Production | **Type:** Anchor Skill (Tier 3)

## 📋 Implementation Protocol

1. **Physically Based Materials:** Enforce Roughness, Metalness, and Normal-Mapping consistency.
2. **Global Illumination:** Simulate complex bounce-lighting to achieve 99% photorealism.
3. **Post-Processing:** Apply cinematic grain and color-grading for the "Workspace Factory" aesthetic.

## 🌍 Regional Calibration (MENA Context)

- **Climate Simulation:** Adjust Kelvin temperatures to match regional sun patterns (5500K-6500K).
- **Material Standards:** Focus on render-fidelity for luxury materials (Gold, Marble, Polished Steel).

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

- **Anti-Pattern:** Plastic Material Look -> *Correction:* Increase specular depth and material roughness variation.
- **Anti-Pattern:** Artificial Lighting -> *Correction:* Use IBL (Image Based Lighting) from real regional HDRI environments.

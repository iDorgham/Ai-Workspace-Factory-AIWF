## 1. Executive Summary
This phase defines the **Information Architecture (IA)** and **Content Strategy** for the AIWF industrial website. It follows the **SDD (Software Design Document) Method**, treating content as a structural component governed by strict specifications and high-fidelity mapping.

---

## 2. Structural Requirements (REQ-CONTENT)

### [REQ-CONTENT-001] — Hierarchical Sitemap
- **AC**: Define a 3-tier deep sitemap covering Home, Pillars, and Deep Library documentation.
- **AC**: Ensure 100% crawlability and logical parent-child relationships.

### [REQ-CONTENT-002] — Menu Flow & UX
- **AC**: Map the primary navigation (Mega-Menu) and footer topology.
- **AC**: Define "Quick-Action" paths for industrial commands (/plan, /dev).

### [REQ-CONTENT-003] — Component Content Types
- **AC**: Specifications for Hero, Feature-Grid, Industrial-Table, and Metadata-Block components.

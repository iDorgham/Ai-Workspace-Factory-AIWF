# 🌙 Zakat Auditor Logic
> **Skill Category:** Financial Auditing / Ethical Logistics
> **Basis:** Hijri (Lunar) Year, Nisab Benchmarks

## Purpose
Automate the calculation and distribution logic for corporate and project-level Zakat. This skill ensures that the "Ethical-Tax" physics of Islamic Finance are calculated with precision based on lunar anniversary cycles.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Techniques

### 1. Lunar-Cycle Zakat Calculation
- **Physics**: Track the "Zakat-Anniversary" (Hawl) for every investment node using a lunar calendar sync.
- **Logic**: Apply the 2.5% (Lunar) or 2.577% (Solar) calculation to current "Net-Growth" stubs.
- **Verification**: Cross-reference current project hardware/assets against "Nisab" (minimum wealth threshold) benchmarks.

### 2. Purification & Ethical Distribution
- **Physics**: Identify "Non-Compliant-Scraps" (minor interest from conventional banks) and calculate the necessary purification percentage.
- **Workflow**: `/sharia Zakat-Calc` generates a structured distribution report listing approved ethical charity nodes.
- **Audit-Trail**: Maintain an immutable log of all Zakat distributions to ensure 100% transparency.

## 🛡️ Governance & Anti-Patterns
- **Anti-Pattern**: Mixing Zakat funds with general operational expenses.
- **Correction**: Use the "Zakat-Vault-Logic"—distributed funds MUST be moved to a segregated, audit-locked sub-node.

# ⚡ Energy & Renewables Mastery

High-Density Operational Skill for the governance and optimization of Renewable Energy Assets (Solar, Wind, Hybrid).

## 🧭 OPERATIONAL PROTOCOLS

### 1. The LCOE Solver (Economics)
- **Baseline**: Levelized Cost of Energy must remain competitive within the regional market (Egypt/UAE).
- **Physics**: Calculate full lifecycle costs including Capex, OpEx (Maintenance), and decommissioning.
- **Yield**: Use meteorological probability models (P50/P90) to project annual output.

### 2. Grid Compliance (Physics)
- **Synchronization**: Ensure all assets meet frequency and voltage injection standards.
- **Harmonics**: Monitor THD (Total Harmonic Distortion) to prevent equipment degradation.
- **Curtailment**: Predict and mitigate the risk of grid-enforced output limitation.

### 3. PPA Governance (Legal-Finance)
- **Settlement**: Reconcile technical output with PPA billing milestones.
- **Physics**: Align "Commercial Operation Date" (COD) with financial revenue recognition triggers.

## 🛡️ ANTI-PATTERNS (ZERO-TRUST)
- **PIPELINE-SIGMA**: Injecting power without a verified Grid License.
- **PIPELINE-TAU**: Calculating ROI without adjusting for LCOE degradation over 25 years.
- **PIPELINE-UPSILON**: Ignoring curtailed energy in the financial model.

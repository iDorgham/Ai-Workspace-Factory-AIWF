# 📈 Revenue Yield Physics
> **Skill Category:** Financial Optimization / Yield Management
> **Domains:** Luxury Lodging, Inventory Distribution

## Purpose
Enforce the economic physics of hospitality yield management. This skill ensures that the property maintains optimal RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room) without compromising brand prestige.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Techniques

### 1. ADR & RevPAR Orchestration
- **Physics**: Automate the calculation of Average Daily Rate (ADR) based on real-time room stubs.
- **Auditing**: Trigger "Yield-Alerts" if RevPAR falls below the 5-star baseline for >48 hours.
- **Workflow**: `/hotel YieldReport` generates a matrix of current revenue versus seasonal projections.

### 2. Dynamic Inventory Distribution
- **Physics**: Orchestrate the inventory flow between direct booking nodes and Third-Party (OTA) nodes (Expedia, Booking.com).
- **Logic**: Implement "Prestige-Pricing"—automatically cap the maximum inventory released to lower-tier discount channels.

---

## 🛡️ Governance & Anti-Patterns
- **Anti-Pattern**: Drastic price-slashing for last-minute occupancy.
- **Correction**: Use the "Yield-Hard-Floor" protocol—all dynamic pricing must stay within the brand's luxury standard deviation.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 100% of room inventory is synchronized across all distribution nodes.
- [ ] RevPAR health score maintained within the "Optimal" zone (>90%).

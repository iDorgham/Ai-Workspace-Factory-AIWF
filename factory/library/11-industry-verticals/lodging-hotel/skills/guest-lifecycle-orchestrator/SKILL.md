# 🧬 Guest Lifecycle Orchestrator
> **Skill Category:** Hospitality Operations / Guest Physics
> **Domains:** Luxury Lodging, Reservation Systems

## Purpose
Govern the technical lifecycle of a hospitality guest. This skill ensures that the guest journey from reservation to check-out is industrialized, frictionless, and OMEGA-consistent.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Techniques

### 1. Reservation & Check-in Physics
- **Physics**: Draft and maintain structured Guest Stubs (Booking ID, Preferences, Loyalty Status).
- **Automation**: Execute "Arrival-Prep" logic—verify room readiness stubs 2 hours before the guest's timestamp-omega.
- **Verification**: Cross-check guest identification hashes against internal security registries.

### 2. Guest Feedback & Retentional Logic
- **Logic**: Automated sentiment scrapers for post-stay feedback.
- **Workflow**: `/hotel BookingIndex` identifies repeat-guest nodes and triggers "VIP-Elite" welcome protocols.
- **Protocol**: Generate a "Guest Profile Hash" to preserve preferences across multiple property stays.

---

## 🛡️ Governance & Anti-Patterns
- **Anti-Pattern**: Manual property check-in without digital stub generation.
- **Correction**: Use the "Omni-Arrival" protocol—all check-ins MUST be recorded with a bi-directional guest-staff timestamp.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 100% of guest stays result in a structured digital Guest Stub.
- [ ] Arrival-ready verification score of 100/100.

# 🏗️ Corporate Entity Architect
> **Skill Category:** Structural Physics / Governance
> **Constitutions:** DIFC, ADGM, Mainland UAE (Companies Law), Egypt (GAFI)

## Purpose
Design and govern corporate structures across complex jurisdictions. This skill maps the **Principal-Agent** logic between holding entities and mainland subsidiaries while ensuring 100% compliance with UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) and ESR (Economic Substance Regulation) mandates.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Techniques

### 1. Structural Physics Mapping
- **Onshore/Offshore Sync**: Design structures where DIFC/ADGM SPVs hold mainland assets.
- **Principal-Agent Mapping**: Explicitly define the separation of powers between the Board, Shareholders, and Executive Management.
- **Workflow**: `/legal StructureMap` generates a visual graph of ownership percentages and board control.

### 2. Statutory Compliance Automation
- **UBO Tracking**: Maintain a persistent register of beneficial owners (>25% shareholding) for mandatory GAFI/MoE filings.
- **ESR Detection**: Monitor business activities and trigger "Economic Substance" reporting requirements if "Relevant Activities" (e.g., Banking, Insurance, Fund Management) are detected.
- **Renewal Watchdog**: Sentinel agent monitors Trade License and Establishment Card validity.

---

## 🛡️ Governance & Anti-Patterns
- **Anti-Pattern**: Using nominee shareholders without a formalized trust agreement.
- **Correction**: Enforce the "Nominee-Node" block—all nominee structures MUST have a linked, hashed legal trust deed in the workspace.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] UBO register is 100% compliant with local Ministerial Decrees.
- [ ] Structural diagrams reflect correct legislative boundaries (Civil Law vs Common Law).
- [ ] Zero trade license expirations under Sentinel watch.

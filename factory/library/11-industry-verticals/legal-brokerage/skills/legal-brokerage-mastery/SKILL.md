# ⚖️ Legal Brokerage Operations (MENA)

## Purpose
Enforce professional operational and technical standards for Legal Brokerages and Law Firms in the MENA market. This skill focuses on **Litigation Lifecycle Management**, **Corporate Entity Structuring**, and **Legal Operations Automation**.

---

## Technique 1 — Litigation & Case Lifecycle Management

### The "Evidence-First" Protocol
- **Case Indexing**: Standardize the indexing of all case files using a "Court-Ready" taxonomy. Ensure all evidence is timestamped and cryptographically hashed to maintain chain-of-custody for Dubai/Cairo courts.
- **Hearing Orchestration**: Automate the scheduling of hearings and the "Drafting Deadlines" for memorandums. Ensure that all filings are submitted via the national digital justice portals (e.g., Dubai Courts' "E-Litigation" system) at least 48 hours before the deadline.

## Technique 2 — Corporate Entity Structuring & Governance

- **Structural Physics**: Design corporate structures based on the **Principal-Agent** logic. Map the relationship between Onshore/Freezone entities for tax and labor optimization (e.g., DIFC SPVs holding mainland assets).
- **Statutory Compliance**: Automate the tracking of Trade License renewals, UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) filings, and ESR (Economic Substance Regulation) reporting.

## Technique 3 — SAFT/SAFE Physics & Venture Capital
- **Post-Money SAFE Logic**: Audit investment contracts for mandatory clauses: Valuation Cap, Discount Rate, Governing Law, and Pro-Rata rights.
- **Instrument Synthesis**: Automate the generation of a structural physics summary for SAFTs. Ensure Token Generation Event (TGE) readiness clauses are verified.
- **Bilingual NDAs**: Assemble bilingual (Arabic/English) Non-Disclosure Agreements for Dubai/Cairo jurisdictions using the local standard template.

## Technique 4 — Legal Operations (LegalOps) Automation
- **Document Assembly**: Implement template-based assembly for standard contracts (NDAs, MOUs, Employment Agreements) using the `skills:04-business-strategy/business/egypt-contract-drafting-physics` baseline.

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missing Filing Deadlines** | Dismissed Case | Use a "Two-Tier" alert system—one for the Lawyer and one for the Admin/Sentinel to ensure 100% compliance with court dates. |
| **Weak Data Privacy** | Legal Liability | Never transmit sensitive Case Data via standard unencrypted email; utilize a secure "Client Portal" for all correspondence. |
| **Outdated Templates** | Invalid Contract | Schedule a quarterly "Legal Audit" to update all templates based on the latest Ministerial Decrees (e.g., the new UAE Labor Law of 2022). |

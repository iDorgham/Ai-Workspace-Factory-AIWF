# 🍔 F&B Operations (MENA)

## Purpose
Enforce professional operational and technical standards for the Food & Beverage (F&B) sector in the MENA market. This skill focuses on **Menu Engineering**, **COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) Tracking**, and **Cloud-Kitchen Logistical Physics**.

---

## Technique 1 — Menu Engineering & Strategic Pricing

### The "Stars & Dogs" Standard
- **Profit vs. Popularity**: Analyze sales data to categorize every menu item:
  - **Stars**: High Profit, High Popularity.
  - **Plowhorses**: Low Profit, High Popularity (Needs price adjustment).
  - **Puzzles**: High Profit, Low Popularity (Needs marketing).
  - **Dogs**: Low Profit, Low Popularity (Delete from menu).
- **Physics of Layout**: Place "Star" items in the "Eye-Capture Zone" (top-right for English, top-left for Arabic) of the physical/digital menu to increase gross margin by > 10%.

---

## Technique 2 — COGS Tracking & Procurement Physics

- **Inventory Transparency**: Implement real-time tracking of ingredients down to the "Gram/Unit" level. Link the POS (Point of Sale) to the Inventory system so that every sale automatically deducts the ingredient-component from stock.
- **Waste Governance**: Perform daily "Waste Audits." In MENA climates, focus on cold-chain integrity for perishables to reduce food waste to < 5% of total stock.

---

## Technique 3 — Cloud-Kitchen & Delivery Logistics

- **The "30-Minute" Window**: For cloud-kitchen entities, prioritize the "Delivery Radius." Optimization for Talabat, Deliveroo, and HungerStation must ensure that the "Prep-to-Pickup" time is < 12 minutes to maintain food temperature.
- **Kitchen Flow Physics**: Design kitchen layouts for "Line Efficiency," minimizing the steps required for a chef to move from the cold-prep station to the grill to the packaging node.

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Guessing Food Costs** | Margin collapse | Calculate the "Theoretical Food Cost" (TFC) monthly and compare against the "Actual Food Cost" (AFC) to identify theft or waste issues. |
| **Poor Delivery Packaging** | Negative sentiment | Utilize high-grade insulated or ventilated packaging to ensure that fried foods remain crispy during a 20-minute transit. |
| **Ignoring Local Seasonality** | Excess inventory | Adjust procurement orders during Ramadan or Summer when daytime dine-in traffic drops by > 40-60%. |

---

## Success Criteria (F&B QA)
- [ ] COGS is tracked and maintained within +/- 3% of theoretical model.
- [ ] Menu Engineering (Stars & Dogs) report is completed quarterly.
- [ ] Average Prep-to-Pickup time for delivery is < 12 minutes.
- [ ] Food Waste is verified and logged at < 5%.

# 🛒 E-commerce & Retail Operations (MENA)

## Purpose
Enforce professional operational and technical standards for the E-commerce and Retail sectors in the MENA market. This skill focuses on **Supply-Chain Resilience**, **Conversion-Rate Optimization (CRO)**, and **Omni-channel Loyalty Physics**.

---

## Technique 1 — Supply-Chain & Fulfillment Resilience

### The "Last-Mile" Standard
- **Regional Hubbing**: Utilize local fulfillment centers (e.g., UAE's JAFZA, KSA's MODON) to maintain a "Safety Stock" of top 20% high-velocity SKUs (Stock Keeping Units). Ensure that delivery within major cities (Dubai, Riyadh, Cairo) is achievable within < 24 hours.
- **Inventory Velocity tracking**: Automate the calculation of "Reorder Points" based on the last 30 days of sales velocity to prevent "Out-of-Stock" (OOS) conditions which destroy SEO and ranking physics on sites like Noon or Amazon.ae.

---

## Technique 2 — Conversion-Rate Optimization (CRO) Physics

- **The "High-Trust" Checkout**: In MENA regions, "Cash on Delivery" (COD) is still prominent but shifting. Always offer a multi-tiered checkout: Apple Pay, Credit Card, and a Tabby/Tamara "Buy Now Pay Later" (BNPL) option to increase average order value (AOV) by > 20%.
- **Localized Cart Recovery**: Use WhatsApp Business API for automated cart recovery. Since WhatsApp open-rates in MENA exceed 90%, it is 5x more effective than email for recovering abandoned checkouts.

---

## Technique 3 — Omni-channel Loyalty & Engagement Loops

- **The "Phygital" Bridge**: Ensure that loyalty points earned in-store (Physical) are instantly mirrored in the user's online profile (Digital). 
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Logic**: Use RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis to segment users. Send "High-Tension" offers to "At-Risk" segments to prevent churn and maintain a healthy growth trajectory.

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ignoring COD Fees** | Margin leakage | Factor in the "Return-to-Origin" (RTO) costs associated with Cash-on-Delivery when pricing low-margin goods. |
| **Non-Mobile Optimized** | 70-80% bounce rate | In MENA, > 85% of e-commerce traffic is mobile. Optimize page-load speeds to < 2.5 seconds (Lighthouse). |
| **Generic Arabic Translation** | Brand trust collapse | Use "Marketing Arabic" (Modern Standard or targeted Dialect) rather than raw machine translation to ensure product descriptions feel premium and local. |

- [ ] Omnichannel loyalty points sync is verified across POS and Web.
- [ ] Cart recovery (WhatsApp) is active and achieving > 15% recovery rate.

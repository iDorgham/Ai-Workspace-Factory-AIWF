# 🎼 Electronic Composition & Theory (Omega-tier)

## Purpose
Enforce standards for composing modern electronic music (Techno/House). This skill focuses on **Modal Scales**, **MIDI Rhythmic Precision**, and the **Psychology of Tension & Release.**

---

## Technique 1 — Modal Foundations (Genre-Specific)

### Melodic Techno & Indie Dance Scales
- **Techno Core**: Prioritize **Phrygian**, **Dorian**, and **Aeolian** (Natural Minor) scales to create darker, more moody atmospheres.
- **Tension Tones**: Utilize the **Flat 2nd** (Phrygian) or **Flat 5th** (Locrian-ish) to create dissonant tension before resolving to the tonic.

---

## Technique 2 — Rhythm & The "Beat" Physics

### MIDI Quantization & Groove
- **Rule**: Never use 100% hard quantization. Apply "Groove Templates" (e.g., MPC/Swing) to the velocity and timing of the Hi-Hats and Percussion to avoid a "Robotic" feel.
- **Kick-Bass Relationship**: The "Heartbeat." Ensure the rhythmic relationship is either "On-the-floor" (1234) or "Syncopated" (Gallop) to drive the track energy.

---

## Technique 3 — Tension & Release Architecture

- **The Build (Ascension)**: Utilize automation on Filter Cutoff, White Noise, and Reverb Size to increase sonic density.
- **The Drop (The Zenith)**: Release all low-pass filtering and re-introduce the Kick/Sub-bass at maximum impact.
- **Silence as a Tool**: Use 1-2 beats of complete silence (The "Void") right before the drop to maximize the perceived energy shift.

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Over-complex Harmony** | Cluttered Mix | In Techno, "Less is More." Focus on one strong melodic motif and evolve it through synthesis/rhythm rather than chord changes. |
| **Static Velocity** | No Groove | Use MIDI "Velocity Shapers" or manual editing to vary the intensity of every hit. |
| **Abrupt Transitions** | Loss of energy | Use "Ear Candy" (one-shot FX, risers) to bridge the gap between 16 or 32-bar sections. |

---

## Success Criteria (Composition QA)
- [ ] Track follows a logical 16/32-bar developmental cycle.
- [ ] Scale choice is consistent across all melodic instruments.
- [ ] Groove/Swing feels natural and contributes to the "Drive."
- [ ] Tension build-up results in a distinct, high-energy release.

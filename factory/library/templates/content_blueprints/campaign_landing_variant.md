## STRUCTURE BLUEPRINT

### Section 1: Hero
- Purpose: one clear promise + one CTA.
- Length: 20-40 words.

### Section 2: Pain-to-Outcome Block
- Purpose: map the core problem to measurable result.
- Length: 90-140 words.

### Section 3: Offer Snapshot
- Purpose: what is included, timeline, and fit criteria.
- Length: 120-180 words.

### Section 4: Proof Stack
- Purpose: 3 proof points (project/result/testimonial stub).
- Length: 80-130 words.

### Section 5: Final CTA
- Purpose: repeat same CTA with low-friction reassurance.
- Length: 30-60 words.

## SEO + CRO CHECKLIST

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1 and metadata.
- [ ] Single CTA text reused in hero and footer.
- [ ] At least one location/market signal included.
- [ ] No navigation-heavy copy; conversion flow remains focused.

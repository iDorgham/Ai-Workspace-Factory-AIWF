# 🌉 Cross-Vertical Bridge
> **Skill Category:** Meta-Orchestration / Sector-Integration
> **Version:** 1.0.0

## Purpose
Govern the logical intersections between different industry verticals within the OMEGA-LIB. This skill provides the master schemas and orchestration logic for multi-sector projects.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Intersections

### 1. Bridge Alpha: Medical Tourism
- **Intersection**: `hospital-clinic` [Medical] x `lodging-hotel` [Hospitality]
- **Physics**: 
  - Synchronize Patient stubs with Guest stubs (ID-Hash parity).
  - Triggers "Luxury-Recovery-Amenities" in the hotel node based on the medical report node.
  - Automates transport-omega between clinical and lodging facilities.

### 2. Bridge Beta: Ethical Clinical-Law
- **Intersection**: `islamic-finance` [Finance] x `hospital-clinic` [Medical] x `legal-brokerage` [Legal]
- **Physics**:
  - Audit medical malpractice contracts for Sharia-compliant "Charity-Penalty" clauses.
  - Enforce AAOIFI-standard insurance logic for clinical risk pools.
  - Ensure ethical purification of non-Sharia revenue in hospital networks.

### 3. Bridge Gamma: Premium Asset Ops
- **Intersection**: `islamic-finance` [Finance] x `lodging-hotel` [Hospitality] x `real-estate-dev` [Real Estate]
- **Physics**:
  - Asset-backed Sukuk logic for hotel development nodes.
  - Ethical "Revenue-Yield" auditing to ensure hotel operations match Sharia benchmarks.

## 🛡️ Governance & Conflict Resolution
- **Anti-Pattern**: Logic clash where a Medical directive violates a Financial Sharia constraint.
- **Protocol**: If a logic clash is detected, the `ShariaReviewer` and `MedicalDirector` agents trigger a joint arbitration loop.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 100% data-parity between intersecting sector stubs.
- [ ] Zero un-audited "Revenue Leaks" in cross-vertical nodes.

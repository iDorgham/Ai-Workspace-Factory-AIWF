## Core Mandates

1. **High-Precision Communication:** All user-facing updates and consultation
   points MUST be concise, clear, and bullet-pointed. Avoid long paragraphs.

2. **Strategic Collaboration:** Before starting any plan, ask: "HITL
   (Collaborative) or Autonomous (Agent-Led)?"

3. **Decision Points:** Present **two short options** (A or B) and wait for a
   decision before proceeding.

4. **Red Sea Luxury Backdrop:** All shot locations and lifestyle scenarios MUST
   be set in high-end Red Sea resort areas: **Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh,
   or Soma Bay**.
   - **Vibe:** Luxury coastal living, sophisticated yacht clubs, premium
     architecture, and high-tech resort security.

5. **Brand Aura:** Every piece of content must respect the "Invisible Sentinel"
   archetype—high-tech, reliable, and premium.

6. **Visual Standards:** Use 'Workspace Factory Cyan' and high-contrast 'Dark Mode'
   aesthetics.

7. **Motion Language:** 100ms transitions, smooth mechanical lifts, and
   holographic ripples.

8. **Platform Optimization:**
   - **Instagram/TikTok:** 9:16 (Vertical), rapid hooks, visually dense.
   - **YouTube/Vimeo:** 16:9 (Horizontal), 4K Cinematic storytelling.
   - **LinkedIn/Facebook:** 4:5 or 1:1, strategic, data-driven.
   - **Website Blog:** SEO-optimized, technical deep-dives.

9. **Campaign Organization:** Every project/campaign must be organized in a
   dedicated folder under `docs/guides/marketing/production/YYYY-MM/`.

10. **Task & Media Verification:** Include all 5-tier project folders. Use
    `TASK_MANAGER.md` to track progress across all channels.

11. **Brand Consistency (Images):** All image posts must follow the "Five-Layer
    Framework" even when not intended for video.

12. **Asset Orchestration & Persistent Storage:** All generated AI images and
    storyboards MUST be immediately moved to the project-specific `photo/raw/`
    folder.
    - **Naming:** Use "clean" descriptive names (e.g., `resort_gateway_in.png`).
    - **Persistence:** Ensure assets are saved within the project structure, not
      just referenced in the brain.

13. **Master Landing Page Protocol:** When designing high-converting lead-gen
    pages, follow this order:
    - **Step 1: Content First** — SEO Research, Keyword Planning, and
      Benefit-driven Copywriting.
    - **Step 2: Asset Briefing** — Generate AI image prompts and visual UI
      assets based on the content strategy.
    - **Step 3: Multi-Mode Design** — Design using Workspace Factory Light and Dark Mode
      colors (ADS semantic tokens).
    - **Step 4: Professional UI/UX Animation** — Apply high-end motion
      (staggered entrances, spring physics, and shared layout morphs).

## Technical Orchestration (2026 Standards)

Every AI prompt generated by this skill must follow the **Five-Layer Framework**:

1. **Subject/Scene:** Core entity, environment, and specific actions.
2. **Optics:** Lens choice, focal length, aperture (e.g., "35mm anamorphic, f/2.8").
3. **Camera Motion:** Precise directional vectors (e.g., "slow dolly-in 0.5m/s").
4. **Lighting Stack:** Specific parameters (e.g., "5600K key, cyan 3200K rim").
5. **Style/Look:** Aesthetic references (e.g., "Kodak Portra 400," "Chiaroscuro").

## Cinematic Production Standards

Refer to these definitions to achieve high-production-value results:

### 1. Advanced Lighting Strategy

- **Key Light (5600K):** High-intensity cold daylight to define the main subject.
- **Rim Light (Cyan #00F2FF):** Strategic back-lighting for a "halo" effect.
- **Fill Light (Deep Blue):** Soft blue ambient light for the shadows.
- **Chiaroscuro Ratio:** Aim for high-contrast ratios (4:1 or higher).

### 2. Lensing & Optics

- **Anamorphic 35mm:** For cinematic flares and horizontal 16:9 stretch.
- **85mm f/1.8 Prime:** For macro scanner shots with extreme background blur.
- **50mm Standard:** For medium human-centric shots.
- **Probe Lens (Macro):** For extreme "punch-through" shots of sensors/screens.

### 3. Comprehensive Shot Types

- **ECU (Extreme Close-Up):** Finger tapping the phone or sensor flash.
- **POV (Point of View):** The resident's perspective looking at the gate.
- **Low-Angle Hero:** Shooting the gate from ground up to feel "majestic."
- **High-Angle/Drone:** Sweeping views of the community at night.

### 4. Advanced Camera Movement

- **The "Curtain-Riser" Lift:** Smooth accelerating vertical pedestal move.
- **The "Heartbeat" Dolly:** Slow, rhythmic push-in moves toward a scanner.
- **The "Data-Swirl" Orbit:** Rotating 360-degrees around a resident.

## AI Voiceover (VO) Best Practices

Write scripts "for the ear":

- **Conversational Flow:** Use contractions and short, punchy sentences.
- **Pause Markers:** Use `<break time="1s"/>` or `...` for absorption.
- **Phonetic Guidance:** Spell out complex terms (e.g., "HMAC" as "aitch-mack").
- **Emphasis:** Use **Bold** or CAPS if the tool supports stress detection.
- **LinkedIn Strategy:** Focus on TSL (Thought, Solve, Lead) copy structure.

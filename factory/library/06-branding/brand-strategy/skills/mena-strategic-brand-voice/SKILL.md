# 🧬 MENA Strategic Brand Voice

> **Governance:** 03-Creative | **Sector:** 🏢 Branding | **Type:** Anchor Skill (Tier 3)

## 📋 Implementation Protocol

1. **Archetype Alignment:** Map the brand to a regional archetype (e.g., The Sage, The Hero) adjusted for MENA values.
2. **Linguistic Layering:** Define the exact balance of formal (Fusha) and colloquial (Ammiya) Arabic.
3. **Visual Resonance:** Sync the tone of voice with the "Cine-Serious" visual framework.

## 🌍 Regional Calibration (MENA Context)

- **Honorifics:** Use appropriate regional honorifics and business address protocols.
- **Value Anchoring:** Anchor the brand promise in trust, loyalty, and hospitality (regional trust triggers).

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

- **Anti-Pattern:** Translated Slang -> *Correction:* Use natively authored regional metaphors.
- **Anti-Pattern:** Overly Casual Tone -> *Correction:* Use "Respectful Authority" as the default baseline.

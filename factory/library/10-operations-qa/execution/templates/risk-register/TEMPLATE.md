## How to use (5×5 matrix)

**Likelihood × Impact** → **Score** (1–25). Re-score after mitigations.

| Score | Action bias |
|-------|-------------|
| 20–25 | Immediate mitigation + escalation |
| 12–19 | Plan mitigation + owner + date |
| 6–11 | Monitor + document |
| 1–5 | Accept or watchlist |

# 📈 Growth Economics & ROI Physics

## Purpose
Enforce professional standards for the financial governance of advertising spend. This skill focuses on the **Mathematical Models of Growth**, focusing on **Targeting Logic**, **Lead Generation Velocity**, and the **Physics of Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).**

---

## Technique 1 — The ROAS Physics (Unit Economics)

### LTV / CAC (Life Time Value vs. Cost Per Acquisition)
- **Goal**: Maintain a ratio of > 3:1. If the cost to acquire a customer exceeds 1/3 of their lifetime value, the growth is "Unstable."
- **Payback Period**: Aim for a "Payback" (breaking even on the acquisition cost) within the first 3-6 months.

---

## Technique 2 — Lead Generation & Funnel Velocity

- **Lead Velocity Rate (LVR)**: Measure the percentage growth in qualified leads month-over-month.
- **Conversion Gap Analysis**: Identify where potential leads are dropping off (Ad Click -> Landing Page -> Form Submission -> Sale).
- **Optimization**: If the Landing Page conversion is < 5%, apply A/B testing specifically to the "CTA (Call to Action)" and "Hero Image."

---

## Technique 3 — Targeting & Audience Logic

- **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Mapping**: Use data-driven targeting (Lookalike audiences, Interest clusters) to avoid "Broad Spend" which dilutes ROI.
- **Intent vs. Interest**: Prioritize **Intent-based** traffic (Google Search) for immediate sales and **Interest-based** traffic (Meta/Social) for upper-funnel awareness.

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Scaling Unqualified Traffic** | Wasted spend | Always optimize for "Sales/Conversions," not just "Clicks." |
| **Ignoring Churn** | Negative LTV | Factor "Customer Retention" into the ROI calculation; high CAC is only sustainable with low Churn. |
| **Single-Channel Dependency** | Growth fragility | Maintain a diversified "Media Mix" (e.g., 60% Search, 30% Social, 10% Offline). |

---

## Success Criteria (Economics QA)
- [ ] ROAS is tracked and exceeds the sector baseline (e.g., > 3x).
- [ ] Lead Velocity shows consistent monthly growth.
- [ ] Multi-touch attribution is active to avoid over-crediting a single channel.
- [ ] LTV/CAC ratio is calculated and verified quarterly.

# 📸 Ad Creative Production Mastery

## Purpose
Enforce professional standards for creating high-performance advertising assets. This skill focuses on the **Technical Production** of photography and videography, establishing standards for **In-House Studio** setups and the **Strategic Direction of External Agencies**.

---

## Technique 1 — The In-House Studio Standard

### Hardware & Lighting Physics
- **Lighting**: Use a "3-Point Lighting" setup as the baseline (Key, Fill, Back). For product photography, use "Softboxes" and "Reflectors" to eliminate unwanted specular highlights.
- **Camera Configuration**: Professional-tier mirrorless (Sony A7-series or Canon R-series) with "Prime Lenses" (35mm or 50mm) for maximum sharpness and low-depth-of-field separation.
- **Sound (Video)**: Always use and external "Lavalier" or "Shotgun" mic; ad audio quality is the #1 indicator of brand professionalism.

---

## Technique 2 — Directing External Agencies (Strategy)

### The "Sentinel" Creative Brief
- **Protocol**: Never start a project without a comprehensive brief including:
    1. **Primary Objective**: (e.g., Lead Gen for Real Estate).
    2. **Platform Constraints**: (Sizes: 1080x1080 for Feed, 1080x1920 for Stories).
    3. **Conversion Hook**: The first 3 seconds must contain the "Value Proposition."
- **Review Loop**: Use "Stage Gates" (Moodboard -> First Cut -> Final Grade) to ensure the agency doesn't drift from the brand book.

---

## Technique 3 — Advertising Design & Sizes (Dynamic Patterns)

### Ad Set Uniformity
- **Asset Variations**: Every campaign must have a minimum of 3 variations:
    1. **Static Image** (Clean product/service shot).
    2. **Carousel** (Process/Benefit narrative).
    3. **Short-form Video** (UGC or High-end Cinematic).
- **Safe Zones**: Ensure text and "Call to Action" buttons are within the "Safe Zones" of social platforms (avoiding UI overlap).

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Burying the Hook** | High skip rate | Put the most important information in the thumbnail and first 3 seconds. |
| **Low-Contrast Text** | Unreadable on mobile | Use high-contrast brand tokens and bold typography for "Readability at a Glance." |
| **Generic Stock Imagery** | Brand dilution | Always prioritize "In-House" or "Custom Commissioned" assets over generic stock. |

---

## Success Criteria (Production QA)
- [ ] Asset dimensions match platform-native requirements (no 404/mismatched sizes).
- [ ] Technical quality (Audio/Video bitrates) follows professional delivery standards.
- [ ] Safe zones are verified for all social placements.
- [ ] Briefs include unambiguous ROI and conversion targets for the creative.

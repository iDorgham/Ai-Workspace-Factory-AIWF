# 📊 Google & Meta Ads Mastery (Omega-tier)

## Purpose
Enforce professional standards for dominating platform-native advertising managers. This skill focuses on the **Algorithmic Mechanics** of Google (PMax, Search) and Meta (Advantage+, Facebook/Instagram), ensuring maximum conversion efficiency using native AI tools.

---

## Technique 1 — Google Ads (The Intent Machine)

### Performance Max (PMax) Optimization
- **Rule**: Garbage IN = Garbage OUT. Populate PMax with high-density visual assets (per `skills:17-performance-marketing-growth/ad-creative-production/ad-creative-production-mastery`).
- **Signal Strategy**: Feed "Customer Match" lists and "Search Intent" clusters to jumpstart the algorithm's learning phase.

### Search Mastery (Negative Keywords)
- **Protocol**: Apply "Negative Keyword" lists vigorously. Ensure you are not bidding on your own brand name unless specifically required for defensive positioning.

---

## Technique 2 — Meta Ads (The Emotional Disruptor)

### Advantage+ & Ad Sets
- **Campaign Structure**: Use "Broad Targeting" combined with "Advantage+ Creative" — let the Meta AI find the audience based on how they interact with the creative.
- **Dynamic Creative (DCO)**: Use up to 10 images and 5 headlines per ad set; the algorithm will auto-select the highest-performing combination.

---

## Technique 3 — A/B Testing & Scaling

- **The "Winner" Protocol**: When an ad set achieves a ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) > 3.0, increase the daily budget by 20% every 48 hours. DO NOT double the budget instantly; it breaks the algorithm's stable state.
- **Creative Refresh**: Rotate hooks every 14 days to prevent "Ad Fatigue."

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Over-Segmentation** | Fragmented data | Consolidate ad sets into larger "Learning" pools to give the AI more data. |
| **Ignoring Post-Click** | High bounce/Low ROAS | Verify the "Landing Page" matches the "Ad Promise" exactly (Conversion congruency). |
| **Missing Pixel Tracking** | Data blindness | Verify CAPI (Conversions API) and Meta Pixel before spending $1. |

---

## Success Criteria (Native Ads QA)
- [ ] Pixel/CAPI tracking is active and verified.
- [ ] Ad sets have > 50 conversions per week (Learning phase complete).
- [ ] ROAS is tracked at the Campaign level.
- [ ] Negative keyword lists are applied to all Search campaigns.

# 📻 Offline Media & Negotiation Mastery

## Purpose
Enforce professional standards for purchasing and managing traditional advertising surfaces. This skill focuses on the **Economics of Attention** for Billboards, TV, Radio, and Magazines, with a specialization in **Contracting & Negotiation Logic**.

---

## Technique 1 — Billboard & Outdoor (OOH) Logistics

### Placement Physics (Reach & Frequency)
- **Visibility Index**: Evaluate Billboard locations based on "Drive-by volume" and "Dwell Time" (e.g., Highway vs. Traffic Light intersection).
- **Format Standardization**: Ensure all creative assets map to the specific mechanical requirements (e.g., Unipole, Megacom, or Digital LED Screen).

---

## Technique 2 — TV & Radio (Broadcast) Excellence

### Programmatic TV vs. Spot Buying
- **Spot Buying**: Negotiating specific "Time Slots" during high-rated shows (e.g., 9 PM prime time).
- **Targeting**: Align Radio ads with specific demographic listening blocks (e.g., Morning commute for business professionals).

---

## Technique 3 — The Negotiation Framework (Media Buying)

### Contracting Logic
- **The "Sentinel" Discount**: Always push for 20-30% below the "Rate Card" price; Traditional media has high perishability—remnant space must be sold.
- **Bonus Impressions**: Negotiate "Value-Addition" (e.g., mention in social posts or extra "rotation" spots) as part of the primary contract.
- **Termination Clauses**: Ensure the ability to "Pause" or "Exit" the media spend if the ROI metrics (measured via tracked URLs/QR codes) fail to meet the baseline.

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Buying "Static" Rate Cards** | Over-spending | Everything in traditional media is negotiable. Never pay the first price. |
| **Untracked Offline Spend** | ROI Blindness | Use **QR Codes**, **Unique Coupon Codes**, or **Specific Phone Numbers** for every billboard/spot to trace the lead back. |
| **Poor Contrast (Legibility)** | Wasted impressions | For Billboards: Use high-contrast colors and max 7 words; users only have 3 seconds of focus. |

---

## Success Criteria (Offline QA)
- [ ] Media contracts reflect > 20% savings against rate cards.
- [ ] Tracking mechanisms (QR/Unique code) are active and verified.
- [ ] Placement visibility is manually or photographically verified.
- [ ] TV/Radio scripts are timed to the exact second (no cut-offs).

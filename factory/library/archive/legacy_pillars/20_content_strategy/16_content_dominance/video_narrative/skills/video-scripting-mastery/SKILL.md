# 🎬 Video Scripting Mastery (Short & Long Form)

## Purpose
Enforce professional standards for scriptwriting across conflicting platform cultures. This skill optimizes for **Short-form Virality** (TikTok/Reels) and **Long-form Authority** (YouTube/Documentaries), ensuring every frame serves the narrative goal.

---

## Technique 1 — Short-Form Virality (TikTok/Reels)

### The "Scroll-Stop" Architecture
1. **The Hook (0-2s)**: Use a visual or auditory disruption (e.g., "The industry is lying to you...").
2. **The Context (2-5s)**: Rapid establish why the viewer should care.
3. **The Value (5-45s)**: High-speed delivery with "Visual Jump-cuts."
4. **The Loop-Back (Final s)**: End with a question or a "Circular Hook" that leads back to the start of the video.

---

## Technique 2 — Long-Form Authority (YouTube/Docs)

### The "Deep-Dive" Structure
- **ACT 1: The Hook & Promise**: Establish the problem and the "Stakes."
- **ACT 2: The Journey/Conflict**: Introduce the "Enemy" (a problem, a competitor, or a myth) and the steps to overcome it.
- **ACT 3: The Payoff**: Deliver the "Omega-tier" solution and a call-to-adventure (CTA).
- **Rule**: Use **"Information Gaps"** — mention a topic early that only gets resolved at the end to maintain high retention.

---

## Technique 3 — Regional Scripting (MENA/Masri)

- **Engagement**: Scripts must include "Street-Level" relatability for the Egyptian market.
- **Tone**: Friendly, high-energy, and inclusive.
- **Language**: Use code-switching (AR/EN) for tech/design topics to resonate with the modern Arab expat and local professional.

---

## 🛡️ Critical Failure Modes (Anti-Patterns)

| Anti-Pattern | Result | Correction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Boring Introductions** | 80% Drop-off | Start at the "Peak" of the emotion/event. |
| **Linear Pacing** | Loss of interest | Use "Curiosity Loops" every 60 seconds in long-form. |
| **No CTA** | Zero Conversion | Always give the viewer a "Physical Action" (Follow, Click, Comment). |

---

## Success Criteria (Script QA)
- [ ] Hook is tested against "Curiosity Gap" logic.
- [ ] Retentions metrics (simulated) project > 50% watch-time at the half-way mark.
- [ ] B-roll and visual cues are explicitly written in the script margin.
- [ ] Script is "Ear-Tested" for national and regional rhythmic flow.
